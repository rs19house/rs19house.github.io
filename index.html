The Fragrance Collection

Each RS19 fragrance carries its own identity, presence, and story. The collection is designed to be experienced, remembered, and shared.

Malik

A commanding fragrance created to express authority, confidence, presence, and disciplined strength.

Nur

A fragrance inspired by light, clarity, refinement, elegance, and the power of illumination.

Sakinah

A fragrance shaped around peace, balance, warmth, comfort, and quiet spiritual confidence.

Qadr

A fragrance representing destiny, value, purpose, distinction, and the weight of divine measure.

The RS19 Fragrance Experience

RS19 creates interactive fragrance activations that allow guests to smell, compare, vote, respond, and become part of the story.

Smell

Guests experience a curated collection of RS19 fragrances through a professionally presented sampling station.

Vote

Participants use a mobile survey to rate fragrances, select favorites, and provide measurable feedback.

Share

Participants may submit family-and-friend reaction videos and help extend the fragrance experience beyond the venue.
